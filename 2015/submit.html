Camera-Ready Paper Submission Instructions

Submit your camera-ready paper via EDAS.

Submission policies and formatting guidelines for camera-ready paper versions are given below.

Formatting Guidelines: Papers should be prepared using the IEEE conference template, in two-column, single-spaced, 10pt format (see here for templates). Papers should be submitted as portable document format (PDF), formatted for letter-size paper (8.5" x 11"). Fonts should be embedded. Margins should be 0.7" for top, left, and right, and 1" for the bottom.

Paper length policy: Final papers must be six pages in length or less.

Attendance policy: All papers will be presented orally at the workshop. At least one author of each paper is expected to attend the workshop and present the paper. In line with IEEE policies, we reserve the right to exclude "no show" papers from IEEE Xplore.

Registration policy: At least one author of each paper must register for the workshop at the regular (non-student) rate. Each registration will cover up to two papers.
