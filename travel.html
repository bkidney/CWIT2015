Accommodation

CWIT 2015 has arranged special accommodation rates at three venues for the duration of the conference.

On Campus

Memorial University Campus Housing Rooms are available on Memorial University campus at a rate of $45.00 per night (regular rate) and $40.00 per night (student rate). This rate includes 4% hotel tax and 13% HST. It includes a single room (twin-sized mattress) with a semi-private washroom. Linens and towels are provided. Access to the common areas on the floor (kitchenette/tv room, and study area) on the floor is also included for conference participants.

Downtown St. John's

Downtown St. John's hotels are a great way to experience St. John's in between conference events. These venues are within walking distance of many attractions but will require a car (rental or taxi) to get to and from the conference venue. Sheraton Hotel Newfoundland Rooms are available at the Sheraton Preferred rate of $199 + taxes and fees per night (base on single/double occupancy, $15 per additional guest to a maximum of 4). Rooms include complimentary wifi, parking, and access to the fitness centre, pool and hot tub. To secure your reservation, please contact the Sheraton In-House Reservations Department directly via e-mail atreservations@sheratonhotelnewfoundland.comand note the group name, CDN Workshop on Information Theory Conference. Our In-House Reservations Department is also open Monday – Friday, 9:00 am – 6:00 pm (Newfoundland Standard time), please call 709-758-8453 or Toll Free at 1-888-870-3033. Delta St. John's Rooms are available at the Delta for the following rates.
  • Delta Room - $199.00/night
  • Premier Room - $219.00/night

  • Suites - $279.00 - $699.00/night
Guest room rate includes complimentary basic wireless internet in guest rooms and public areas as well as access to the indoor pool, sauna, whirlpool, exercise room and two regulation size squash courts. To make a reservations call 1-888-793-3582 and quote “Canadian Workshop on Information Technology CWIT” or “GCCWIT715”.

Visiting Canada

You may need a VISA to enter Canada. Information regarding who needs to obtain a temporary Visa to visit Canada is outlined at: http://www.cic.gc.ca/english/visit/index.asp

For a letter of invitation, we will need the following information in order to properly prepare that letter:

  • Complete name
  • Date of birth
  • Address and telephone number for where you are staying
  • Dates when you are entering and leaving Canada

Please allow sufficient time when requesting the letter in order to have your application for VISA to be approved.

The VISA application form can be obtained at http://www.cic.gc.ca/english/pdf/kits/forms/IMM5257E.PDF
